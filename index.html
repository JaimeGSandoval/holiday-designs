About

I help elderly and disabled people decorate the interior of their houses for the holidays. It can be challenging for people who are limited in their movement to create an atmosphere for the current holiday, so I thought it would be a good idea to offer my services to them. I have experience with themes and design, and I'd love to use that experience to decorate your house. I simply use whatever decorations you have available to create a delightful arrangement that'll get you in the holiday mood.
