About

I help elderly and disabled people decorate the interiors of their houses for the holidays. It can be challenging for people who are limited in their movement to create an atmosphere for the current holiday, so I thought it would be a good idea to offer my services to them. I have experience with themes and design, and I'd love to use that experience to decorate your house. I simply use whatever decorations you have available to create a delightful arrangement that'll get you in the holiday mood.

How it Works

It's easy to get the interior of your house decorated for the holidays. All you have to do is make an appointment by phone or email, and then I will simply visit your house and use the decorations you have available to create a festive atmosphere for your home. That's all there is to it!
